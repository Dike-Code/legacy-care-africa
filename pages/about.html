Our Story

Built from conviction. Growing by care.

Legacy Care Africa was born from a simple belief: that African families deserve healthcare that honors their identity, their traditions, and their right to dignity at every stage of life.

The Founding Story

Why Legacy Care Africa exists

Legacy Care Africa was founded after witnessing firsthand the gaps that exist between the healthcare African families receive and the care they deserve. Too many mothers birth without a trusted guide. Too many elders spend their final years in facilities far from their children. Too many families navigate complex medical systems alone, in languages that don't feel like home.

"We didn't build Legacy Care Africa to compete with existing healthcare systems. We built it to fill the spaces those systems have always left behind."

Our founders — with backgrounds spanning clinical nursing, public health, financial management, and community development — came together with a shared conviction: healthcare built by Africans, for Africans, within African communities is the most powerful tool for generational wellness we have.

We began with a question: What if every mother in Kenya could access the same quality of birth support as mothers in London or Nairobi's private clinics — regardless of postal code? What if every family caring for an aging parent had access to a nurse, not just a prayer and a hope?

Legacy Care Africa Group is our answer. Through our registered subsidiaries Uzazi Wellness Care and Nyumbani Support Solutions, we are building the infrastructure for the healthcare experience every African family deserves.

What Guides Us

Mission, Vision & Values

01

Mission

To deliver compassionate, culturally grounded healthcare that bridges traditional African healing wisdom with modern medical excellence — ensuring every family we serve feels seen, honored, and well cared for.

02

Vision

A continent where geography, income, or cultural identity never determines the quality of care a family receives — where every birth is dignified, every elder is honored, and every home is a place of healing.

🌱

Ubuntu

We are because you are. Our care is communal, relational, and built on human connection — not transactions.

🤲

Dignity First

Every patient is treated as a person with a history, a culture, and a right to be fully seen and respected.

🔬

Clinical Excellence

Warmth without rigor is not enough. We hold our clinical team to the highest evidence-based standards.

🌍

Pan-African Pride

We celebrate the richness of African cultures in everything we do — language, ritual, food, and relationship.

Our Journey

Building the legacy, one step at a time

2022

The Founding Vision

Legacy Care Africa Group is conceptualized by a team of healthcare professionals, community advocates, and business leaders committed to redefining African healthcare delivery.

2023

Uzazi Wellness Care Registered

Our maternal and birth services subsidiary is officially registered. The first cohort of midwives and birth educators is trained and certified.

2024

Nyumbani Support Solutions Launched

Home care and medical equipment rental services begin operations in Nairobi. The first 50 families receive care within the first month.

2025

Expansion to Mombasa & Kisumu

Both subsidiaries expand to coastal and western Kenya. A diaspora partnership program is launched for Kenyans abroad arranging care for family at home.

2026 →

East African Expansion Underway

Legacy Care Africa Group begins feasibility studies in Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. The vision is continental. The foundation has been laid.
