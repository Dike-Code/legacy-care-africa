Nyumbani — the Swahili word for home — is our commitment to dignified care beyond hospital walls. We bring skilled nurses, compassionate caregivers, and essential medical equipment to where healing matters most: your home.

Home Care Services

Dignified care wherever you call home

👩‍⚕️

Home Nursing Care

Registered nurses providing wound care, medication management, IV therapy, catheter care, and chronic disease monitoring in your home — hourly, daily, or residential.

Elder Care Support

Compassionate caregivers providing personal hygiene assistance, meal preparation, mobility support, companionship, and memory care for aging family members.

🩺

Post-Surgical Recovery

Structured recovery programs for patients discharged from hospital after surgery — including dressing changes, physiotherapy coordination, and pain management monitoring.

💊

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care plans for diabetes, hypertension, COPD, heart failure, and kidney disease — including regular monitoring, medication adherence support, and patient education.

🧠

Palliative & Respite Care

Gentle, dignity-centered care for patients in late-stage illness — and rest care for family caregivers who need a break without compromising their loved one's comfort.

🏃

Physiotherapy at Home

Certified physiotherapists offering stroke rehabilitation, orthopedic recovery, fall prevention, and mobility restoration programs in the home setting.

Getting Started

From inquiry to care in 24 hours

1
Call or book online

Our care coordinators are available 24/7. Describe your situation and we begin the matching process immediately.

2
Free home assessment

A senior nurse visits your home to assess care needs, environment safety, and equipment requirements — at no cost.

3
Personalized care plan

A written care plan outlines services, schedule, assigned caregiver, and goals. You approve it before care begins.

4
Care begins

Your caregiver arrives on schedule. Equipment is delivered and set up. Ongoing supervision by our clinical team.

5
Regular review & adaptation

Weekly check-ins with you and the care team. We adapt the plan as your loved one's needs evolve.
