🌿 Uzazi Wellness Care

Where Birth
Becomes Sacred Again

Uzazi — the Swahili word for childbirth — is our commitment to restoring dignity, power, and joy to every birth journey. We are a maternal wellness company for African mothers, by African caregivers.

Our Philosophy

Birth is not a medical event — it's a sacred passage

Modern medicine has saved countless lives — and we honor that. But birth has always been a spiritual, communal, and deeply feminine experience in African tradition. At Uzazi, we hold both truths: the clinical and the cultural. Your body knows how to birth. Our role is to support, protect, and celebrate every step.

🤲

Continuity of Care

The same caregiver from your first appointment through your postpartum recovery.

🌍

Culturally Rooted

We honor African birth traditions — language, ritual, family presence, and rest practices.

🧠

Trauma-Informed

No patient is rushed, pressured, or dismissed. Your experience matters as much as the outcome.

📋

Evidence-Based

Clinical best practices from WHO, ACOG, and regional maternal health research guide every protocol.

What We Offer

Full-spectrum maternal wellness

🤱

Prenatal Care Package

12-week to 40-week pregnancy monitoring including ultrasound coordination, nutritional counseling, birth planning sessions, and weekly check-ins with your dedicated midwife.

🏡

Home Birth Support

For low-risk pregnancies, Uzazi's certified midwives attend home births with full emergency equipment, creating a serene, family-centered birth space in your own home.

🌸

Postpartum Wellness

40-day postpartum support including daily nurse visits, lactation guidance, pelvic floor physiotherapy, mental health screening, and new mother nutrition planning.

👩‍⚕️

Midwife-Led Birth Classes

Group and private childbirth education classes covering breathing techniques, pain management, partner support, infant feeding, and newborn care essentials.

💛

Perinatal Mental Health

Specialist support for anxiety, depression, and trauma during pregnancy and after birth. We screen every mother and ensure no one falls through the cracks.

🍼

Lactation & Infant Feeding

Certified lactation consultants offering in-home visits, troubleshooting, and culturally sensitive guidance for breastfeeding and combination-feeding mothers.

Your Journey

What to expect with Uzazi

01

Initial Consultation

A 60-minute welcome session to understand your health history, birth preferences, cultural needs, and goals for your pregnancy journey.

02

Personalized Care Plan

Your dedicated midwife creates a tailored birth plan that honors your wishes, your culture, and evidence-based safety guidelines.

03

Continuous Support

Regular check-ins, 24/7 on-call access to your care team, and monthly group wellness circles with other expectant mothers.

04

Birth Day Care

Your midwife attends your birth — at home, in a birth center, or at a hospital — providing continuous labor support and advocacy for your birth plan.

05

Postpartum Recovery

Daily home visits in the first 2 weeks, then weekly support through 40 days. Physical, emotional, and nutritional care for you and your newborn.

Postpartum Spotlight

The fourth trimester deserves its own chapter

In many African traditions, a new mother rests for 40 days — fed, bathed, and honored by the women around her. Uzazi brings that sacred practice into the modern world. Our postpartum program is not an add-on. It is the center of our care philosophy.

  • ✓ Daily nursing visits for the first 14 days
  • ✓ Lactation consultant on-call 7 days a week
  • ✓ Pelvic floor physiotherapy assessment at 6 weeks
  • ✓ Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale screening
  • ✓ Newborn pediatric check-up coordination
  • ✓ Partner & family support guidance sessions
